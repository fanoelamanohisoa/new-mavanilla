about

We are a group of Bio farmers and collectors all over Madagascar. We have twenty two years experiences on the preparation and the conditioning of vanilla and thirty five in collecting cash products. We embarked on a phase of integrated and well organized production regarding the culture to the management of vanilla beans by joining associations. In 2004, production continues to grow and business size is changing at the same time.

We are responsible of the merchandise until it is on board. Once on board we are not responsible of the merchandise anymore.

Our Main Activities

COLLECTING

On the north-east coast, protected by the lush rainforest, grows the most beautiful of all orchids, tamed by man.

PROCESSING

Processing vanilla consists of a series of six operations: scalding, steaming, drying, the trunk, calibration and packaging

PACKAGING

Vacuum bags 5Kg * 6 = 30Kg per box for vanilla and 60kg or 80kg jute bags for Cloves and Cocoa Beans and others cash products

LOADING

We are responsible of the merchandise until it is on board. Once on board we are not responsible of the merchandise.

SHIPPING

DHL, FEDEX and many other transits availables as per your needs and requirements. We provide all clear documents for your port health custom clearance.
